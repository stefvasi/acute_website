Mohammadmahdi holds BSc and MSc degrees in Biomedical Engineering from Islamic Azad University in Tehran. His doctoral research focuses on the design and development of tactile sensory feedback systems to enhance control and perception in bionic prosthetic limbs, conducted in collaboration with Ossur and funded by the Rannis Technical Development Fund.
